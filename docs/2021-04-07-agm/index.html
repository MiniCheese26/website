2021 AGM - Advert

Wednesday 7 April 2021
1 min

Our 2021 Annual General Meeting will take place at 7.00pm on Thursday 22nd April.

It is an opportunity to come and hear about what we are doing and voice your thoughts about what we should aim for in the next 12 months.

Given the current restrictions for gathering in person, this will be a virtual meeting hosted via Zoom. Members have until noon on the day of the meeting to register for the event. Connection details to attend the meeting via zoom will be sent to those who have signed up after the deadline for registrations. You can register via eventbrite here

Non-members are welcome to join the meeting but must register no later than 24 hours before its start time. You are welcome to listen to the debate but cannot vote at the meeting. If you wish to become a member you can do so FOR FREE via this link
