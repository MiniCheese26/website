May update from Cycling England

Monday 10 May 2010
1 min

The May update from Cycling England includes an on-carriageway cycle parking project in Brighton and Hove. Could this work in Portsmouth?

Cycling England is offering a free LTP review service to Local Authorities. This could be of particularly useful in the planning stage where more detailed advice on potential programmes may be required. For more information follow the link from the DfT cycling home page.

Results from the first three years of England’s first six Cycling Towns are now available on the Cycling England web site (key information, progress so far) and there are updates from the Cycle Journey Planner project including how Local Authorities can get involved.
