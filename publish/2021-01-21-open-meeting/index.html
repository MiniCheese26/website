Portsmouth Cycle Forum

Portsmouth Cycle Forum is a group for anyone who cycles – or wants to cycle – in and around the Portsmouth area.

Open Meeting

Our aim is to bring you up to speed with all the current projects in development whether they are being funded through the Local Transport Plan, South East Hants Rapid Transit projects or are being developed through the wider regeneration of the city.  We hope to have time for some questions, but some of us will probably retire to a local hostelry to discuss the issues raised, if you want to continue the conversation.
